About Me

I am currently pursuing my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering at Er. Perumal Manimekalai College of Engineering, Hosur, with a deep passion for Full Stack Development and Artificial Intelligence.

My journey in technology started with a curiosity to understand how software is built and how complex problems can be solved with code. This project enhanced my understanding of Natural Language Processing (NLP), deep learning models, and the practical application of AI in real-world scenarios.

Beyond academics, I am constantly exploring new tools, frameworks, and emerging trends in technology. I enjoy working on end-to-end solutions, from designing intuitive user interfaces to optimizing backend performance and integrating intelligent features using AI.

I believe in continuous learning, problem-solving, and innovation, and my goal is to contribute to impactful projects that combine software engineering excellence with AI-powered solutions.
